Text-to-Video Generator

Create high-quality videos from your scripts, ideas, or descriptions

Objective

Create a platform where content creators can input a script, description, or idea, and the system automatically generates a high-quality video tailored to the text, with customizable options for visuals, style, music, and more.

How It Works

1. Input Script or Idea

Enter the text you want to transform into a video. Be specific with what kind of visuals, tone, and mood you're aiming for.

Example: 'Create a video explaining how to start a YouTube channel, with a professional yet friendly tone. Include an intro scene with a logo animation, followed by cutaways to a host speaking on camera. Transition to screen recordings showing the YouTube interface and key tools.'

2. Video Style & Theme

Select the style and theme of the video, and customize further with color palettes, lighting, and visual aesthetics.

3. Visuals & Scene Suggestions

The system will suggest visuals for your script, which you can adjust. Option to upload custom images, videos, or logos.

4. Audio & Music

Choose background music and adjust the volume. Option to upload your own music or voiceovers, or generate AI-based voiceovers.

5. Video Length & Format

Adjust the video length and choose the output format (landscape, square, vertical).

6. Advanced Customization (Optional)

Add special effects, annotations, and interactive elements.

7. Preview & Edits

Preview the video, make adjustments, and export in different resolutions.
